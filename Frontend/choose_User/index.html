— The Process
HOW IT WORKS
From SOS tap to hospital bed — under 10 minutes.
01
Patient

One Tap SOS Request

Press the emergency button — your GPS locks instantly and broadcasts your location and condition to the nearest available drivers and partner hospitals in real time. No typing, no waiting.

02
Driver

Nearest Driver Confirms

Certified emergency drivers receive a push alert with the patient's precise coordinates and medical note. The closest available driver confirms pickup in a single tap — en route in seconds.

03
Hospital

Hospital Prepares in Advance

Partner hospitals receive real-time ETA updates and basic patient vitals before arrival — so the right team, room, and equipment are ready the moment the patient walks through the door.

04
All Users

Live Tracking & Transparency

Patients track their driver live. Family members receive a shareable link. Hospitals monitor the ETA dashboard. Every party stays informed at every moment.
