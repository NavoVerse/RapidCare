PERSONAL INFORMATION STEP 1

IDENTITY VERIFICATION (KYC) REQUIRED

Aadhaar card

12-digit unique identification number - UIDAI issued

Pending
+ Front side JPG / PNG / PDF • max 5 MB
+ Back side JPG / PNG / PDF • max 5 MB

PAN card

10-character alphanumeric - Income Tax Department

Pending
+ Upload PAN card image JPG / PNG / PDF • max 5 MB

Driving licence

Must be valid for commercial / transport vehicle (MCWG, LMV-TR, HMV)

Not verified
+ Front of licence JPG / PNG • max 5 MB
+ Back of licence JPG / PNG • max 5 MB
All KYC documents are verified against government databases (UIDAI, NSDL, Sarathi Parivahan). Data is encrypted and stored securely as per IT Act 2000.

VEHICLE DOCUMENTS STEP 3

RC (Registration certificate)

Vehicle registration number

+ Upload RC copy PDF / JPG - max 5 MB

Vehicle insurance

Valid comprehensive / third-party

+ Upload insurance doc PDF / JPG - max 5 MB

Fitness certificate

Motor vehicle fitness (FC)

+ Upload FC document PDF / JPG - max 5 MB

Permit (If applicable)

State / national permit for transport

+ Upload permit PDF / JPG - max 5 MB

VEHICLE PHOTOS ALL ANGLES REQUIRED

+ Front view
+ Rear view
+ Left side
+ Right side
+ Interior / cabin
+ Dashboard
+ Number plate
+ Medical kit

Each photo must be taken in daylight. Min resolution 5MP. JPG/PNG only - max 5 MB each.
